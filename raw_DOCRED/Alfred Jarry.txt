He also coined the term and philosophical concept of ' pataphysics . Jarry was born in Laval , Mayenne , France , and his mother was from Brittany . He was associated with the Symbolist movement . His play Ubu Roi is often cited as a forerunner of Dada , and to the Surrealist and Futurist movements of the 1920s and 1930 . Jarry wrote in a variety of hybrid genres and styles , prefiguring the postmodern . He wrote plays , novels , poetry , essays and speculative journalism . His texts are considered examples of absurdist literature and postmodern philosophy .
