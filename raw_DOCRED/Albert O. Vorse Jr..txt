Rising in rank throughout the war , he would act as Executive Officer and Operations Officer in different squadrons before being given command of his own squadron , VF-80 , the eponymous Vorse 's Vipers . Vorse ended the war as commander of Air Group 80 and ultimately achieved rear admiral rank before his retirement in 1959 .
