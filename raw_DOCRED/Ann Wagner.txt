The district , based in St. Louis County , is heavily suburban and the wealthiest district in the state . It includes most of St. Louis 's southern and western suburbs as well as some of the northern exurbs in St. Charles County and the northern portion of Jefferson County . Wagner is a member of the Republican Party . Previously , she served as the United States Ambassador to Luxembourg from 2005 to 2009 . Prior to her diplomatic post , Wagner was Chair of the Missouri Republican Party for six years , from 1999 until 2005 , and Co - chair of the Republican National Committee for four years .
