He was a dock worker in 1941 when he witnessed the Japanese attack on Pearl Harbor , and would later work as a military photographer for the U.S. Army , serving in World War II , and the Korean War and the Vietnam War . He briefly left the armed forces to work for National Geographic and the Associated Press during the Vietnam War , but then returned to work for the Army during the war .
